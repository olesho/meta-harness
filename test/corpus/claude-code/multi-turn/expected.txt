❯ what is its population                                                                                                
                                                                                                                        
⏺ Paris has a population of about 2.1 million within the city proper, and roughly 11–12 million in the greater          
  metropolitan area (Île-de-France region).

❯ how does that compare to Berlin                                                                                       
                                                                                                                        
⏺ Berlin's city population is about 3.7–3.9 million, which is larger than Paris proper (~2.1 million) — but Berlin      
  covers a much bigger area (~890 km² vs. Paris's ~105 km²), so Paris is far denser. At the metro level the comparison  
  flips: greater Paris has ~11–12 million people, while Berlin's metro area is around 6 million. So Berlin is the bigger
  city by municipal boundaries, but Paris is the much larger urban agglomeration.
